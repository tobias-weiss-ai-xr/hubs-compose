# Hello WebXR: Your Gateway to Immersive Web Experiences

Discover the power of Virtual Reality directly through your web browser with this immersive WebXR experience. Accessible with a single click on any WebXR-enabled browser, this application is a prime example of comfortable VR accessibility.

## Powered by Industry Collaboration

WebXR is the result of a collaborative effort by leading technology companies including Google, Microsoft, and Mozilla. This modern standard, which replaces the limitations of the older WebVR 1.1, enables rich, interactive VR and AR experiences directly within the web.

## Experience the Future of VR

*   **One-Click Access:** Launch the VR experience seamlessly from your browser.
*   **Immersive Interaction:** Explore digital environments with intuitive controls.
*   **Industry Standard:** Built on WebXR, ensuring broad compatibility and future development.

At **GraphWiz**, we are dedicated to supporting open-source initiatives and advancing the XR ecosystem. Explore this showcase to understand the potential of XR integrated with the web.
